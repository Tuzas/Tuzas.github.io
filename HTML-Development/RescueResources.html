Required Resources for Rescue Operations
  • Personnel
    • -Personnel are used to locate people and to provide aid
    • -Without properly organized personnel, the other resources would not be properly utilized
  • Transportation
    • -Transportation is anything that could be used to move relief workers from the staging area into the disaster area
    • -The logistics of transportation is of the utmost importance since its organization will dictate how fast relief workers will begin providing aid
  • Medical Intervention / Medical Supplies
    • -These consist of available medical teams as well as medical supplies available to these medical teams
    • -Early intervention can reduce the amount of deaths
  • Rescue Equipment
    • -Rescue equipment can be anything that is used to locate and extricate victims from rubble
    • -This equipment can include anything from digging tools to rescue dogs that can help look for survivors
  • Staging Area
    • -A staging area is a location where rescue resources and operations can be organized effectively, it is usually outside of the disaster area, however this is not always the case with earthquakes
    • -A properly managed staging area can effectively disperse rescue resources to hard hit areas and coordinate with rescue operations working in the field
  • Temporary Shelter
    • -Temporary shelters will need to be constructed in order to keep people out of the elements. These areas require cleared space that can support large numbers of people.
    • -Rescuing people is only half of the job, ensuring they have a place to stay while rebuilding starts is the other
  • Food and Water
    • -Basic rations for rescued survivors
    • -Some survivors are trapped for days without access to food or water, part of their treatment is providing them with basic rations
  • Air Support
    • This can include helicopters for search and rescue operations and larger airplanes for delivery of resources from other countries
    • Aircraft are one of the fastest forms of transportation, rescue helicopters could be employed to move the most injured victims
